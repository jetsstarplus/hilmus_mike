About MIKE Creatives

What We Do

We are MIKE Creatives; Made In Kenya Exclusively, a Creatives’ Social Enterprise focused on delivering context relevant digital solutions necessary to propel growth and scale the impact of works of Art in Kenya. We believe in the gift of Art and the Creative Economy in Kenya as a catalyst for the attainment of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Below are amongst our list of services;

  • Artist Management
  • Youtube Campaigns
  • Skiza code generation
  • Upload Content to boomplay
